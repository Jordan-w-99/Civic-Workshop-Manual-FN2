Reminder System - Walk-in Switch Test
  1. Remove the driver's or passenger's seat, and disconnect the connectors.
  1. Check for continuity between the No. 1 and No. 2 terminals of the walk-in switch 2P connector (A).
    • With the fold down lever (B) pulled up, there should be continuity.
    • With the fold down lever released, there should be no continuity.
  1. If the continuity is not as specified, replace the walk-in switch.
