Speaker Replacement
Front Speaker
  1. Remove the door panel.
  1. Remove the bolt. Then lift the speaker straight up to release the lower clips (A).
    If you pull the speaker out too far from the door or body panel, you will damage the lower clips.
  1. Disconnect the 2P connector (B), and remove the speaker.
  1. Install the speaker in the reverse order of removal.
Rear Speaker
  1. Remove the rear speaker grill.
  1. Remove the bolt. Then lift the speaker straight up to release the lower clips (A).
    If you pull the speaker out too far from the door, you will damage the lower clips.
  1. Disconnect the 2P connector (B), and remove the speaker.
  1. Install the speaker in the reverse order of removal.
Tweeter
  1. Remove the door mirror garnish (A). Then disconnect the connector (B).
  1. Carefully pry the tweeter grille (A) out of the door mirror garnish. Be careful not to damage the tweeter grille and the door mirror garnish.
  1. Remove the tweeter speaker (B) from the speaker grille.
  1. Install in the reverse order of removal.
