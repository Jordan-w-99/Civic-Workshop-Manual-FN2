i-SHIFT System Symptom Troubleshooting - No transmission power to wheels
No Transmission Power to Wheels
  1. Start the engine.
  1. Press the brake pedal, and change the shift lever to the A/M position.
  1. Check the BRAKE SWITCH in the DATA LIST with the HDS.
Is the brake switch OK?
YES -
Go to Step 4 .
NO -
Do the brake pedal position switch signal circuit troubleshooting; R18A2 engine model, L13Z1 engine model. ■
  1. Check that the gear position indicator is displayed in 1st gear while in Auto mode.
    NOTE: The gear position indicator should not flash. If the gear position indicator flashes, go to Step 5 .
Is the gear position displayed?
YES -
Go to Step 8 .
NO -
Go to Step 5 .

    • Leaks at hoses and line joints and connections, and repair if necessary.
    • Clutch lines for damage and leaks. Also check for bent clutch lines.
    • Reservoir level.

NO -
