Rear Seat Cushion Cover Replacement
NOTE:
  • When removing components, use the trim tools or equivalent.
  • Take care not to tear the seams or damage the seat covers.
  • Put on gloves to protect your hands.
  1. Remove the rear seat.
  1. Disassemble the rear seat.
  1. Release the seat cushion cover clips (A) and hook strips (B) of the seat cushion cover from the undercover, and remove the screws (C).
Right seat cushion
Left seat cushion
  1. Remove the seat cushion undercover (A) from the seat cushion (B).
  1. Separate the seat cushion cover/pad (A) and seat cushion frame (B).
  1. Pull back the edge of the cushion cover all the way around, and release the clips (A), then remove the seat cushion cover.
Right seat cushion
Left seat cushion
  1. Right seat cushion: If necessary, remove the clip, then remove the inner cover (A).
  1. Left seat cushion: If necessary, remove the screw, then remove the cushion pipe cover (A).
  1. Install the cover in the reverse order of removal, and note these items:
    • To prevent wrinkles when installing a seat cushion cover, make sure the material is stretched evenly over the pad before securing the clips, hooks, and hook strips.
    • Replace any clips you removed with new ones (A).
