M/T Differential Oil Seal Replacement
Special Tools Required

Driver handle, 15 x 135L
 07749-0010000

Bearing driver attachment, 52 x 55 mm
 07NAD-P200100

Oil seal driver, 65 mm
 07JAD-PL90100
  1. Remove the oil seal (A) from the transmission housing (B).
    NOTE: Be careful not to damage the transmission housing while removing the oil seals.
  1. Remove the oil seal (A) from the clutch housing (B).
  1. Install the new oil seal in the transmission housing with the 15 x 135L driver handle (A) and the 52 x 55 mm bearing driver attachment (B).
  1. Install the new oil seal in the clutch housing with the 65 mm oil seal driver (A).
