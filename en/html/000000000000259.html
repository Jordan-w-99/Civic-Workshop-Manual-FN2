Power Mirror Replacement
  1. Lower the door glass fully.
  1. By hand, carefully release the clip, lift the cover upward to release the hook (A, B), if equipped with a tweeter, disconnect the connector (C) and remove the mirror mount cover (D) in the sequence shown.
  1. Remove the front door panel.
  1. Disconnect the connector (A), then detach the harness clip (B) and remove the door wire harness (C).
  1. While holding the mirror (A), remove the nuts securing the mirror.
  1. While holding the mirror, pull the connector (B) out through the hole from inside the vehicle, then remove the mirror. Take care not to scratch the door.
  1. Install the mirror in the reverse order of removal, and make sure the connector is plugged in properly.
