Brake Master Cylinder Disassembly
NOTE: The illustrations show LHD type.
  1. Remove the rod seal from the master cylinder.
  1. Disconnect the reservoir tank hose (A).
  1. Remove the snap ring (A) while pushing in the secondary piston (B).
  1. Remove the reservoir union (A) and the reservoir grommets (B) from the master cylinder.
    NOTE: When reservoir union and master cylinder body are separated, replace the reservoir grommets with new ones.
  1. Remove the stop pin (C) while pushing in the secondary piston (D).
  1. Remove the secondary piston (A) and primary piston (B) and piston guide (C).
  1. Remove the reservoir seal (A) from the reservoir cap (B).
  1. Remove the strainer (C) from the reservoir tank (D).
