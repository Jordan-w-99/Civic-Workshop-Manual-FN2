Clutch Pedal Pad Replacement
NOTE:
  • Put on gloves to protect your hands.
  • Wear eye protection while remove the rivets with drill.
  1. Center-punch each of the three rivets (A), and drill (B) their heads off with a drill bit. Be careful not to damage the clutch pedal when removing rivets.
  1. Remove the rivets from the clutch pedal plate (C).
  1. Remove the clutch pedal plate and the clutch pedal pad from the clutch pedal.
  1. Install the new clutch pedal pad and new clutch pedal plate on the clutch pedal.
  1. Install the new rivets (A) on the clutch pedal plate (B).
    NOTE: Rivets must be present and secure.
  1. Check the clutch pedal height.
