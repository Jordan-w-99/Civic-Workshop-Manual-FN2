M/T Clutch Housing Mainshaft Bearing and Oil Seal Replacement
Special Tools Required

Adjustable bearing remover head, 20−40 mm
  07JAC-PH80100

Bearing remover shaft
 07JAC-PH80200

Slide hammer weight
 07741-0010201

Driver handle, 15 x 135L
 07749-0010000

Bearing driver attachment, 42 x 47 mm
 07746-0010300

Bearing driver attachment, 52 x 55 mm
 07746-0010400
  1. Remove the ball bearing (A) from the clutch housing (B) using the 20−40 mm adjustable bearing remover head (C), slide hammer weight (D) and bearing remover shaft (E).
  1. Remove the oil seal (A) from the clutch side. Be careful when removing the seal so the clutch housing is not damaged.
  1. Drive the new oil seal in from the transmission side using the 15 x 135L driver handle (A) and 42 x 47 mm bearing driver attachment (B).
  1. Drive the new ball bearing in from the transmission side using the 15 x 135L driver handle (A) and 52 x 55 mm bearing driver attachment (B).
