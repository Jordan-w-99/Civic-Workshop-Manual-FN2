Sunshade Calibration
NOTE: The sunshade calibration is required when any of the following have occurred:
  • The sunshade motor is replaced.
  • Any component related to the sunshade replaced.
    • -
    Sunshade bracket
    -
    Sunshade cables, etc.
If the sunshade reverse when it is closing:
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. Press and hold the sunshade CLOSE switch until the sunshade stops in fully closed position.
  1. Release the CLOSE switch. Within 5 seconds, press and hold the CLOSE switch again.
    NOTE: The sunshade moves to fully open position, then it moves to fully close position.
  1. Calibration is complete.
If the sunshade stops during operating:
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. Press and hold the sunshade CLOSE switch.
    NOTE: The sunshade will stop while it is operated.
  1. Release the CLOSE switch.
  1. Press and hold the sunshade CLOSE switch until the sunshade stops in fully closed position.
  1. Release the CLOSE switch. Within 5 seconds, press and hold the CLOSE switch again.
    NOTE: The sunshade moves to fully open position, then it moves to fully closed position.
  1. Calibration is complete.
