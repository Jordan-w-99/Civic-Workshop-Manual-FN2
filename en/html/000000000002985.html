Cam Chain Installation
NOTE: Keep the cam chain away from magnetic fields.
  1. Set the crankshaft to top dead center (TDC). Align the TDC mark (A) on the crankshaft sprocket with the pointer (B) on the oil pump.
  1. Remove the crankshaft sprocket.
  1. Set the camshaft to TDC. The ‘‘UP'' mark (A) on the camshaft sprocket should be at the top, and the TDC grooves (B) on the camshaft sprocket should line up with the top edge of the head.
  1. Install the cam chain on the crankshaft sprocket with the colored piece (A) aligned with the TDC mark (B) on the crankshaft sprocket, then install the crankshaft sprocket to the crankshaft.
  1. Install the cam chain on the camshaft sprocket with the pointer (A) aligned with the center of the two colored pieces (B).
  1. Install the cam chain tensioner (A) and the cam chain guide (B).
  1. Install the cam chain tensioner slider, and tighten the bolt loosely.
  1. Apply new engine oil to the sliding surface of the cam chain tensioner slider (A).
  1. Rotate the cam chain tensioner slider clockwise to compress the cam chain tensioner, and install the remaining bolt, then tighten the bolts.
  1. Check the chain case oil seal for damage. If the oil seal is damaged, replace the chain case oil seal.
  1. Remove the all of the old liquid gasket from the chain case mating surfaces, the bolts, and the bolt holes.
  1. Clean and dry the chain case mating surfaces.
  1. Apply liquid gasket (P/N 08C70-K0234M, 08C70-K0334M, or 08C70-X0331S) to the engine block mating surface of the chain case and to the inner threads of the bolt holes.
    NOTE: Apply liquid gasket about 2.5 mm (0.098 in.) diameter bead along the broken line (A).
  1. Apply liquid gasket (P/N 08C70-K0234M, 08C70-K0334M, or 08C70-X0331S) to the oil pan mating surface of the chain case and to the inner threads of the bolt holes.
    NOTE:
    • Apply liquid gasket about 2.5 mm (0.098 in.) diameter bead along the broken line (A).
    • Apply liquid gasket about 5.0 mm (0.20 in.) diameter bead to the shaded area (B).
    • Do not install components if 5 minutes or more have passed after applying the liquid gasket. Instead, remove the old residue and reapply the liquid gasket.
  1. Set the edge of the chain case (A) to the edge of the oil pan (B), then install the chain case on the engine block (C).
    NOTE:
    • When installing the chain case, do not slide the bottom surface onto the oil pan mounting surface.
    • Wait at least 30 minutes before filling the engine with oil.
    • Do not run the engine for at least 3 hours after installing the chain case.
  1. Tighten the chain case mounting bolts. Wipe off the excess liquid gasket on the oil pan and the chain case mating area.
  1. Install the side engine mount bracket (A), then tighten the new nuts (B), and loosely tighten the bolt (C).
  1. Install the ground cable (D).
  1. Remove the air cleaner housing assembly.
  1. Loosen the transmission mounting bolt and nuts (A).
  1. Raise the vehicle on the lift to full height.
  1. Loosen the lower torque rod mounting bolt (A).
  1. Lower the vehicle on the lift.
  1. Tighten the side engine mount mounting bolt and nuts.
  1. Tighten the transmission mounting bolt and nuts.
  1. Raise the vehicle on the lift to full height.
  1. Tighten the lower torque rod mounting bolt.
  1. Lower the vehicle on the lift.
  1. Install the air cleaner housing assembly.
  1. Install the cylinder head cover.
  1. Install the drive belt auto-tensioner.
  1. Install the crankshaft pulley.
  1. Install the water pump pulley.
  1. Install the drive belt.
  1. Tighten the water pump pulley mounting bolts.
  1. Install the engine undercover.
  1. Install the right front wheel.
  1. Do the crankshaft position (CKP) pattern clear/CKP pattern learn procedure.
