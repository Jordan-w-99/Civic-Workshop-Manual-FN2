Lower Arm Removal/Installation
  1. Raise the front of the vehicle, and support it with safety stands in the proper locations.
  1. Remove the front wheel.
  1. For with HID lamp model: Remove the headlight front leveling sensor.
  1. Remove the lock pin (A) from the lower arm ball joint castle nut, then remove the castle nut (B).
    NOTE: During installation, install a new lock pin after tightening the new castle nut.
  1. Disconnect the lower ball joint from the knuckle using the special tool.
  1. On the left side of the vehicle, remove the dynamic damper mounting nut (A) and the dynamic damper (B).
    NOTE:
    • The dynamic damper is equipped with only left side of the lower arm.
    • Use the new dynamic damper mounting nut during reassembly.
  1. Remove the lower arm mounting bolts and special bolt (C), then remove the lower arm (D).
    NOTE:
    • Use the new lower arm mounting bolts and new special bolt during reassembly.
    • During installation, tighten the lower arm mounting bolts and special bolt to the specified torque with the vehicle's weight. Then install the dynamic damper, and tighten the mounting nut to the specified torque.
  1. Install the lower arm in the reverse order of removal, and note these items:
    • Be careful not to damage the ball joint boot when installing the knuckle.
    • Tighten all mounting hardware to the specified torque values.
    • Before connecting the lower ball joint to the knuckle, degrease the threaded section and tapered portion of the ball joint pin, the knuckle connecting hole, the threaded section and mating surface of the castle nut.
    • First install all the components except dynamic damper and mounting nut, and lightly tighten the bolts, then raise the suspension to load it with the vehicle's weight before fully tightening to the specified torque values.
    • Torque the castle nut to the lower torque specification, then tighten it only far enough to align the slot with the ball joint pin hole. Do not align the castle nut by loosening it.
    • Before installing the wheel, clean the mating surface of the brake disc and the inside of the wheel.
  1. Check the wheel alignment, and adjust it if necessary.
  1. For with HID lamp model: Do the headlight learning procedure.
Bushing Replacement
  1. Press out the lower arm (A) with the suitable socket wrench and a hydraulic press, and remove the lower arm from the bushing (B).
    NOTE: Apply the protective tape to the lower arm under the bushing (C) to prevent damage to the lower arm.
  1. Apply a water to the new bushing surface.
  1. Align the angle of the lower arm (A) and the tab portion (B) of the bushing (C). Press in the bushing by hand first.
    NOTE: The installed bushing's tab portion points upward and downward the to the installed lower arm.
  1. Press in the bushing with the 32 mm socket wrench and a hydraulic press.
  1. Adjust the distance between top of the lower arm and the bushing surface to the dimension shown.
    NOTE: Support the lower arm with suitable flange bolt (A) during pressing.
