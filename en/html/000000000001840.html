Starter Removal and Installation
Removal
  1. Disconnect the negative cable from the battery, then disconnect the positive cable.
  1. Remove the engine undercover.
  1. Remove the harness connectors (A) and harness clamp (B), then remove the intake manifold bracket (C).
  1. Disconnect the knock sensor connector (A).
  1. Remove the two bolts (B) securing the starter, then remove the starter from the engine.
  1. Disconnect the starter cable (A) from the B terminal, then disconnect the BLK/WHT wire (B) from the S terminal.
  1. Remove the harness clamp (C).
  1. Remove the starter.
Installation
  1. Install the starter cable (A) and connector (B). Make sure the starter cable crimped side of the ring terminal is facing out.
  1. Install the harness clamp (C).
  1. Install the starter (A), and tighten the bolts.
  1. Connect the knock sensor connector (B).
  1. Install the intake manifold bracket (A), then install the harness connectors (B) and harness clamp (C).
  1. Install the engine undercover.
  1. Connect the positive cable to the battery first, then connect the negative cable.
  1. Start the engine to make sure the starter works properly.
  1. Set the clock.
