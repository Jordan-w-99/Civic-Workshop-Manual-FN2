Side Curtain Airbag Replacement
Removal
NOTE:
  • Review the interior trim replacement procedure before performing repair or service.
  • Removal of the side curtain airbag must be performed according to the precautions/procedures.
  • The side curtain airbag system consists of the side curtain airbag module, including the roof trim and front grab handle. After the side curtain airbag has deployed, replace these parts.
  1. Disconnect the negative cable from the battery, and wait at least 3 minutes before beginning work.
  1. Remove the headliner.
  1. Disconnect the floor wire harness 2P connector (A) from the side curtain airbag.
Removal
NOTE:
  • Review the interior trim replacement procedure before performing repair or service.
  • Removal of the side curtain airbag must be performed according to the precautions/procedures.
  • The side curtain airbag system consists of the side curtain airbag module, including the roof trim and front grab handle. After the side curtain airbag has deployed, replace these parts.
  1. Disconnect the negative cable from the battery, and wait at least 3 minutes before beginning work.
  1. Remove the headliner.
  1. Disconnect the floor wire harness 2P connector (A) from the side curtain airbag.
  1. Remove the mounting bolts (A) from the bracket. Detach the clips (B), then remove the side curtain airbag.
  1. Remove the mounting bolts (A) from the bracket. Detach the clips (B), then remove the side curtain airbag.
Installation
NOTE:
  • Installation of the side curtain airbag must be performed according to the precautions/procedures.
  • If the airbag is frayed, or has only other visible damage, replace it. Do not attempt to repair an airbag.
  • When you install the airbag, make sure it is not twisted, and not caught between the inflator bracket by the bracket bolts.
  • Make sure that the side curtain airbag inflator retainer is installed properly, otherwise the airbag could accidentally deploy and cause damage or injuries.
  1. Place the new side curtain airbag assembly on the side of the roof. Tighten the side curtain airbag mounting bolts (A).
Installation
NOTE:
  • Installation of the side curtain airbag must be performed according to the precautions/procedures.
  • If the airbag is frayed, or has only other visible damage, replace it. Do not attempt to repair an airbag.
  • When you install the airbag, make sure it is not twisted, and not caught between the inflator bracket by the bracket bolts.
  • Make sure that the side curtain airbag inflator retainer is installed properly, otherwise the airbag could accidentally deploy and cause damage or injuries.
  1. Place the new side curtain airbag assembly on the side of the roof. Tighten the side curtain airbag mounting bolts (A).
  1. Connect the floor wire harness 2P connector (A) to the side curtain airbag.
  1. Reconnect the negative cable to the battery.
  1. Connect the HDS, and clear the DTCs.
  1. After installing the side curtain airbag, confirm proper system operation: Turn the ignition switch ON (II); the SRS indicator should come on for about 6 seconds and then go off.
  1. Install all removed parts.
