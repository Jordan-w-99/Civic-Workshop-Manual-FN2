MTF Inspection and Replacement
  1. Raise the vehicle, and make sure it is securely supported. Remove the under cover.
  1. Remove the filler plug (A) and washer (B), check the condition of the fluid, and make sure it is at the proper level (C).
  1. If the fluid is dirty, remove the drain plug (A), and drain it.
  1. Install the drain plug with a new washer, and refill the transmission fluid to the proper level.
    Fluid Capacity
    1.9 L (2.0 US qt, 1.7 Imp qt) at fluid change
    2.0 L (2.1 US qt, 1.8 Imp qt) at overhaul

    Always use Honda Manual Transmission Fluid (MTF). Using engine oil can cause stiffer shifting because it does not contain the proper additives.
  1. Install the filler plug (B) with a new washer. Install the under cover.
