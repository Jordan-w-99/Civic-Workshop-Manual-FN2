Glove Box Removal/Installation
SRS components are located in this area. Review the SRS component locations and the precautions and procedures before doing repairs or service.
NOTE:
  • Put on gloves to protect your hands.
  • Take care not to scratch the glove box and related parts.
  • When removing components, use the trim tools or equivalent.
  • LHD is shown; RHD is symmetrical.
  1. Remove these items:
    • Rear center console
    • Driver's dashboard lower cover
    • Front center console
    • Passenger's dashboard undercover
  1. Open the front door.
  1. Using your finger lift the lower edge of the passenger's side cover (A) by hand to detach the lower clips.
  1. Gently pull out the top edge of the driver's dashboard side cover (A) to detach the clips, then remove the passenger's dashboard side cover.
  1. Open the glove box lid. Remove the screws (A) and clips (B).
  1. From under the dash, remove the screws (A) and bolt (B).
  1. Pull the glove box (A) back by hand to detach the clips, and disconnect the glove box light bulb socket (B), then remove the glove box.
  1. If necessary, release the hooks (A), then remove the refrigerator valve (B).
  1. Install the glove box in the reverse order of removal, and note these items:
    • Check if the clips are damaged or stress-whitened, and if necessary, replace them with new ones.
    • Make sure the light bulb socket is plugged in properly.
    • Push the clips into place securely.
