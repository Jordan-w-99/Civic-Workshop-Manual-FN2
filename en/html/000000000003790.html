Navigation System DTC Troubleshooting: 2706
DTC 2706:
 Gyro Diag: ECU Temp XX °C
NOTE:
  • Before you troubleshoot, make sure to follow the general troubleshooting information.
  • Do this test only when the trunk temperature is between −20 °C (−4 °F) to 70 °C (158 °F).
  1. Clear the hard error code.
  1. Turn the ignition switch to LOCK (0), and then back to ON (II).
  1. Check for the hard error code.
Is DTC 2706 Indicated?
YES -
Replace the navigation unit. ■
NO -
Intermittent failure, the system is OK at this time.■
