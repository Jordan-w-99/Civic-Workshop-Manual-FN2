Rear Center Console Armrest Replacement
For Some Models
NOTE: Take care not to scratch the armrest, center console, and related parts.
  1. Remove the rear center console.
  1. Gently pull out the center console rear cover (A) to release the hooks (B).
  1. Open the armrest (A), pull the pin (B) off the armrest, then separate the armrest and center console (C).
  1. Install the armrest in the reverse order of removal.
