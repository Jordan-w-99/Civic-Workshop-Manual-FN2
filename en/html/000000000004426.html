DTC Troubleshooting: B1000
DTC B1000:
 Communication Bus Line Error (BUS-OFF)
  1. Clear the DTCs with the HDS.
  1. Turn the ignition switch to LOCK (0) and then back to ON (II).
  1. Wait for at least 6 seconds.
  1. Check for DTCs with the HDS.
Are DTC B1000, B1008, and B1011, and B1032 all indicated?
YES -
Go to Step 5 .
NO -
Intermittent failure. The communication bus line is OK at this time. Check for loose or poor connections.■
  1. Turn the ignition switch to LOCK (0).
  1. Disconnect the appropriate connector at each control unit in the table.

    Appropriate Connector

    Under-dash fuse/relay box connector Q (16P)

  1. Turn the ignition switch to ON (II).
  1. Clear the DTCs with the HDS.
  1. Turn the ignition switch to LOCK (0), and then back to ON (II).
  1. Wait for at least 6 seconds.
  1. Check for DTCs with the HDS.
Are DTCs B1000, B1008, B1011, and B1032 all indicated?
YES -
Faulty MICU; replace the under-dash fuse/relay box. ■
NO -
Go to Step 12 .
  1. Turn the ignition switch to LOCK (0).
  1. Check for continuity between the under-dash fuse/relay box connector Q (16P) terminal No. 6 and body ground.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Repair a short to ground in the wire between the under-dash fuse/relay box connector and the affected control unit.■
NO -
Go to Step 14 .
  1. Turn the ignition switch to ON (II).
  1. Measure voltage between the under-dash fuse/relay box connector Q (16P) terminal No. 6 and body ground.
Is there voltage?
YES -
Repair a short to power in the wire between the under-dash fuse/relay box connector and the affected control unit.■
NO -
Go to Step 16 .
  1. Turn the ignition switch to LOCK (0).
  1. Reconnect the under-dash fuse/relay box connector Q (16P) and the gauge control module (tach) connector (36P).
  1. Turn the ignition switch to ON (II).
  1. Clear the DTCs with the HDS.
  1. Turn the ignition switch to LOCK (0) and then back to ON (II).
  1. Wait for at least 6 seconds.
Are DTCs B1000, B1008, B1011, and B1032 all indicated?
YES -
Replace the gauge control module (tach).■
NO -
Go to Step 22 .
  1. Turn the ignition switch to LOCK (0).
  1. Reconnect the heater control unit/climate control unit 32P connector.
  1. Turn the ignition switch to ON (II).
  1. Clear the DTCs with the HDS.
  1. Turn the ignition switch to LOCK (0) and then back to ON (II).
  1. Wait for at least 6 seconds.
Are DTCs B1000, B1008, B1011, and B1032 all indicated?
YES -
Replace the heater control unit / climate control unit .■
NO -
Go to Step 28 .
  1. Turn the ignition switch to LOCK (0).
  1. Reconnect the immobilizer-keyless control unit 7P connector.
  1. Turn the ignition switch to ON (II).
  1. Clear the DTCs with the HDS.
  1. Turn the ignition switch to LOCK (0) and then back to ON (II).
  1. Wait for at least 6 seconds.
Are DTCs B1000, B1008, B1011, and B1032 all indicated?
YES -
Replace the immobilizer-keyless control unit .■
NO -
Replace the Hands Free Telephone control unit .■
