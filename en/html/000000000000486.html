SRS DTC Troubleshooting: F1-11, F2-11, F3-11, F4-11
DTC F1-11, F2-11:
 Airbags, Side Airbags, Side Curtain Airbags, Seat Belt Tensioners, and/or Seat Belt Buckle Tensioners Deployed
DTC F3-11, F4-11:
 Airbags, Side Airbags, Side Curtain Airbags, and Seat Belt Tensioners Deployed
NOTE: Before doing this troubleshooting procedure, review SRS Precautions and Procedures.
The SRS unit must be replaced after any airbags and/or tensioners have deployed.
