DTC Troubleshooting: P0882 (139-3)
DTC P0882:
 TCM Power Input Voltage Lower than ECM Power Input Signal
NOTE: Before troubleshooting, check for Fuel and Emissions systems DTCs. If any DTCs are indicated, troubleshoot the indicated DTCs first.
  1. Turn the ignition switch to ON (II).
  1. Clear the DTCs with the HDS.
  1. Turn the ignition switch to LOCK (0).
  1. Turn the ignition switch to ON (II), and wait for at 1 second.
  1. Turn the ignition switch START (III).
  1. Check for DTCs with the HDS.
Is DTC P0882 indicated?
YES -
Go to Step 7 .
NO -
Intermittent failure, the system is OK at this time.■
  1. Turn the ignition switch to LOCK (0).
  1. Disconnect the clutch actuator subharness 10P connector.
  1. Turn the ignition switch to ON (II).
  1. Measure the voltage between clutch actuator subharness 10P connector terminal No. 2 and body ground.
Is there battery voltage?
YES -
Remove the clutch actuator assembly; R18A2 engine model, L13Z1 engine model, then check for the clutch actuator subharness, if the wire harness is OK, substitute a known-good TCM, then recheck. If the symptom/indication goes away with a known-good TCM, replace the original TCM; R18A2 engine model, L13Z1 engine model. ■
NO -
Go to Step 11 .
  1. Check the No. 3 (10 A) fuse in the under-dash fuse/relay box.
Is the fuse OK?
YES -
Repair an open in the wire between the No. 3 (10 A) fuse in the under-dash fuse/relay box and the clutch actuator subharness 10P connector.■
NO -
Go to Step 12 .
  1. Remove the No. 3 (10 A) fuse from the under-dash fuse/relay box.
  1. Check for continuity between clutch actuator subharness 10P connector terminal No. 2 and body ground.
Is there continuity?
YES -
Repair a short in the wire between the No. 3 (10 A) fuse in the under-dash fuse/relay box and the clutch actuator subharness 10P connector, and replace the No. 3 (10 A) fuse.■
NO -
Remove the clutch actuator assembly; R18A2 engine model, L13Z1 engine model, and check for clutch actuator subharness. If the wire harness is OK, substitute a known-good TCM, then recheck. If the symptom/indication goes away with a known-good TCM, replace the original TCM; R18A2 engine model, L13Z1 engine model. ■
