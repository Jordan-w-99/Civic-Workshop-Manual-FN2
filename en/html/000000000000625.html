Crankshaft Pulley Removal and Installation
Special Tools Required

Handle
 07JAB-001020B

Crankshaft pulley holder
 070AB-RJA0100

Socket wrench, 19 mm
 07JAA-001020A
Removal
  1. Remove the right front wheel.
  1. Remove the engine undercover.
  1. Remove the drive belt.
  1. Hold the pulley with the handle (A) and holder (B).
  1. Remove the bolt with a 19 mm socket (C) and breaker bar, then remove the crankshaft pulley.
Installation
  1. Clean the crankshaft pulley (A), crankshaft (B), bolt (C), and washer (D). Lubricate with new engine oil as shown.
    ○:
    Clean
    •:
    Lubricate with new engine oil

  1. Install the crankshaft pulley, and hold the pulley with the handle (A) and holder (B).
  1. Tighten the bolt to 69 N·m (7.0 kgf·m, 51 lbf·ft) with a torque wrench and 19 mm socket (C). Do not use an impact wrench.
  1. Tighten the pulley bolt an additional 90 °.
  1. Install the drive belt.
  1. Install the engine undercover.
  1. Install the right front wheel.
