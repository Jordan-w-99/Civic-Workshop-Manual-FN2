DTC Troubleshooting: P19F5 (153-23)
DTC P19F5:
 Select Actuator Motor Rotating Pulse Sensor Signal Circuit High Voltage
  1. Turn the ignition switch to ON (II).
  1. Clear the DTCs with the HDS.
  1. Turn the ignition switch to LOCK (0).
  1. Turn the ignition switch to ON (II).
  1. Check for DTCs with the HDS.
Is DTC P19F5 indicated?
YES -
Go to Step 6 .
NO -
Intermittent failure, the system is OK at this time.■
  1. Turn the ignition switch to LOCK (0).
  1. Disconnect the select actuator motor 6P connector.
  1. Turn the ignition switch to ON (II).
  1. Measure voltage between select actuator motor 6P connector terminal No. 3 and body ground.
Is there battery voltage?
YES -
Repair a short to power in the wire of the change actuator subharness B.■
NO -
Remove the clutch actuator assembly; R18A2 engine model, L13Z1 engine model, and check for change actuator subharness A. If the wire harness is OK, replace the transmission shift change actuator; R18A2 engine model, L13Z1 engine model. ■
