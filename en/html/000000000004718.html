DTC Troubleshooting: P19FE (160-25)
DTC P19FE:
 Clutch Contact Position Adaptation Learning Value Out of Range
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. Clear the DTCs with the HDS.
  1. Do the i-SHIFT System Learning (Static Mode).
  1. Check for DTCs with the HDS.
Is DTC P19FE indicated?
YES -
Go to Step 5 .
NO -
Intermittent failure, the system is OK at this time.■
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF.
  1. Do the CLUTCH FLUID AIR BLEEDING in the ADJUSTMENT MENU with the HDS.
  1. Do the i-SHIFT System Learning (Static Mode).
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF.
  1. Check for DTCs with the HDS.
Is DTC P19FE indicated?
YES -
Go to Step 10 .
NO -
The system is OK at this time.■
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF.
  1. Remove the clutch pipe assembly from the clutch actuator.
  1. Do the CLUTCH FLUID FILLING in the ADJUSTMENT MENU with the HDS.
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. Check for DTCs with the HDS.
Is DTC P19FE indicated?
YES -
Replace the TCM. ■
NO -
Check the slave cylinder and release system.■
