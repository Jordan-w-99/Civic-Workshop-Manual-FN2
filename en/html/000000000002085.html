DTC Troubleshooting: P19FD (160-24)
DTC P19FD:
 Shift Change Actuator Position Learning Value Out of Range
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. Clear the DTCs with the HDS.
  1. Do the i-SHIFT System Learning (Static Mode).
  1. Check for DTCs with the HDS.
Is DTC P19FD indicated?
YES -
Go to Step 5 .
NO -
Intermittent failure, the system is OK at this time.■
  1. Clear the DTCs with the HDS.
  1. Test-drive for 10 minutes.
  1. Check for DTCs with the HDS.
Is DTC P19FD indicated?
YES -
Do the i-SHIFT System Learning (Static Mode), and recheck. If DTC P19FD is stored again, replace the transmission shift change actuator. ■
NO -
The system is OK at this time.■
