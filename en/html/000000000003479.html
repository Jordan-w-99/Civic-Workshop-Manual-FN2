DTC Troubleshooting: P0919 (162-6)
DTC P0919:
 Transmission Shift Change Actuator Neutral Position Learning Time Out
  1. Turn the ignition switch to ON (II).
  1. Clear the DTCs with the HDS.
  1. Do the i-SHIFT System Learning (Static Mode).
  1. Check for DTCs with the HDS.
Is DTC P0919 indicated?
YES -
Go to Step 5 .
NO -
Intermittent failure, the system is OK at this time.■
  1. Turn the ignition switch to LOCK (0).
  1. Remove the transmission shift change actuator; R18A2 engine model, L13Z1 engine model.
  1. Check if the shift arm (A) moves smoothly.
Does the shift arm move smoothly?
YES -
Replace the transmission shift change actuator; R18A2 engine model, L13Z1 engine model.
NO -
Repair the transmission internal failure.■
