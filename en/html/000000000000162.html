Side Sill Panel Replacement
NOTE: Take care not to scratch the body.
  1. Remove these items:
    • Front wheel arch protector, as needed
    • Rear wheel arch protector, as needed
  1. Remove the side sill panel (A).
    -1.
    Remove the screw (B) and the clips (C).
    -2.
    Slide the side sill panel rearward and remove it. The side clips (D, E) will stay in the body.
    NOTE: To remove the clip C, pry the inner pin up at the edge near the line (F) on its head.
  1. Check for damaged or stress-whitened clips, and if necessary, replace them with new ones.
  1. Install the side clips on the side sill panel.
  1. Hold the panel up, and fit all the side clips into the holes in the body, then push on the panel until the side clips snap into place.
  1. Reinstall the front wheel arch protector and the rear wheel arch protector.
