DTC Troubleshooting: B1183
DTC B1183:
 Gauge Control Module (Tach) Lost Communication with EPS Control Unit (EPS Message)
  1. Clear the DTCs with the HDS.
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF, and then back ON (II).
  1. Wait for 6 seconds or more.
  1. Check for DTCs with the HDS.
Is DTC B1183 indicated?
YES -
Go to Step 5 .
NO -
Intermittent failure, the system is OK at this time. Check for loose or poor connections.■
  1. Check for EPS system DTCs with the HDS.
Is any DTCs indicated?
YES -
Go to the indicated DTCs, then recheck.
NO -
Go to Step 6 .
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF.
  1. Disconnect the gauge control module (tach) 36P connector.
  1. Disconnect the EPS control unit connector C (28P).
  1. Connect the gauge control module (tach) 36P connector No. 1 and No. 19 terminals and body ground with jumper wires.
  1. Check for continuity between the EPS control unit connector C (28P) No. 1 and No. 15 terminals and body ground individually.
Are there continuity?
YES -
Substitute a known-good EPS control unit, and recheck. If the indication goes away, replace the original EPS control unit. If the DTC is still present, replace the gauge control module (tach). ■
NO -
Repair an open in the wire.■
