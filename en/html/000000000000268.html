DVD-ROM Replacement
NOTE: When the DVD-ROM is re-inserted or replaced, a map match must be done.
  1. Turn the ignition switch ON (II).
  1. Open the front cover (A) of the navigation unit (B) located behind the glove box.
  1. Press the EJECT button (C).
  1. Remove the DVD-ROM.
  1. Insert the new DVD-ROM.
  1. Close the front cover. Do not turn the ignition switch OFF until data is downloaded to navigation unit.
    NOTE: After servicing, the front cover must be closed. If you start up the navigation system with the front cover open, the display will indicate. ‘‘Navigation unit door is open or No DVD Disk installed. Please check system''.
