Audio System Symptom Troubleshooting - There is no sound from the left speaker when the USB device is connected
There is no sound from the left speaker when the USB device is connected
NOTE:
  • Check the vehicle battery condition first.
  • Check the connectors for poor connections or loose terminals.
  1. Turn the ignition switch to ON (II).
  1. Check the sound output in AM/FM and CD modes.
Is the sound normal?
YES -
Go to Step 3 .
NO -
Go to no sound is heard from the speaker(s). ■
  1. Turn the ignition switch to LOCK (0).
  1. Disconnect USB adapter unit connector A (14P).
  1. Turn the ignition switch to ON (II).
  1. Measure the voltage between USB adapter unit connector A (14P) terminals No. 1 and No. 2 and body ground individually.
Is there battery voltage?
YES -
Go to Step 7 .
NO -
Repair open in the wire(s) between the USB adapter unit and the audio unit.■
  1. Turn the ignition switch to LOCK (0).
  1. Reconnect USB adapter unit connector A (14P).
  1. Turn the ignition switch to ON (II).
  1. Measure the voltage between USB adapter unit connector A (14P) terminal No. 11 and body ground.
Is there less than 0.1 V?
YES -
Go to Step 11 .
NO -
Repair open in the wire(s) between the USB adapter unit and the audio unit.■
  1. Turn the ignition switch to LOCK (0).
  1. Disconnect USB adapter unit connector A (14P) and audio unit connector C (14P).
  1. Check for continuity between terminals of USB adapter unit connector A (14P) and body ground according to the table. Then check for continuity between the same terminals listed in the table and USB adapter unit connector A (14P) terminal No. 4 (The harness shield).
    USB adapter unit connector
    Wire color
    Audio L+
    A6
    BLU
    Audio L−
    A14
    PUR

Is there continuity?
YES -
There is a short in the wire(s) between the USB adapter unit and the audio unit. Replace the affected shielded harness.
NO -
Go to Step 14 .
  1. Check for continuity between USB adapter unit connector A (14P) and audio unit connector C (14P) according to the table.
    USB adapter unit connector
    Audio unit connector
    Wire color
    AUDIO L+
    A6

YES -
Replace the USB adapter unit. ■
