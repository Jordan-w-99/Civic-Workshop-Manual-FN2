Power Mirror Actuator Replacement
Removal
  1. Remove the mirror holder, the power mirror, and disconnect the 10P connector.
  1. Record the 10P connector terminal location and wire harness color.
  1. Disassemble the power mirror 10P connector (A), and remove the terminals from it.
  1. Remove the gasket (A).
  1. Remove the upper housing (A) from the power mirror actuator (B).
  1. Remove the lower housing (A) and visor (B) from the power mirror actuator (C).
  1. Remove the three TORX screws and power mirror actuator (A).
Installation
  1. Route the wire harness of the new actuator through the hole in the bracket and gasket.
  1. Install the actuator, bracket, harness clip, and gasket in the reverse order of removal.
  1. Install the new actuator terminals into the connector in the original arrangement as shown.
  1. Reassemble in the reverse order of disassembly. Be careful not to break the mirror holder when reinstalling it to the actuator.
  1. Reinstall the mirror assembly to the door.
  1. Operate the power mirror to ensure smooth operation.
