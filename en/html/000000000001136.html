Fuel Filter Removal/Installation
Special Tools Required

Fuel filter wrench
 070AA-RMA0101
  1. Remove the air cleaner.
  1. Disconnect the connectors (A).
  1. Disconnect the hoses (B).
  1. Remove the fuel filter cover (C).
  1. Set the fuel filter (A) to the vice, and attach the fuel filter wrench assy.
  1. Remove the fuel filter water level switch.
  1. Remove the fuel filter.
  1. Install the parts in the reverse order of removal with a new O-ring (B).
    NOTE: When tightening the fuel filter with the fuel filter wrench assy, check the length of your torque wrench, and calculate the reading of the torque wrench should be by using the following formula.
