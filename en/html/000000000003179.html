Starter Removal and Installation
Removal
  1. Disconnect the negative cable from the battery.
  1. Remove the air intake duct.
  1. Remove the dipstick.
  1. Remove the harness clamp (A), then remove the dipstick tube (B).
  1. Disconnect the oil pressure switch connector (C).
  1. Disconnect the starter cable (A) from the B terminal and disconnect the connector (B) from the S terminal.
  1. Remove the two bolts securing the starter, then remove the starter.
Installation
  1. Install the starter to the engine. Connect the starter cable (A) and connector (B). Make sure the starter cable crimped side of the ring terminal faces away from the starter when you connect it.
  1. Install the dipstick tube (A) with new O-ring (B).
  1. Install the harness clamp (C), then connect the oil pressure switch connector (D).
  1. Install the dipstick.
  1. Install the air intake duct.
  1. Connect the negative cable to the battery.
  1. Start the engine to make sure the starter works properly.
