Power Mirrors Function Test
  1. Remove the driver's door panel.
  1. Disconnect the 13P connector (A) from the power mirror switch (B).
  1. Choose the appropriate test based on the symptom:
    • Both mirrors don't work, go to Step 4 .
    • Left mirror doesn't work, go to Step 6 .
    • Right mirror doesn't work, go to Step 7 .
    • Retractable mirrors don't work, go to Step 8 .
Both mirrors
  1. Measure voltage between the No. 2 terminal and body ground with the ignition switch ON (II).
    There should be battery voltage.
    • If there is no battery voltage, check for:
      • -
      Blown No. 36 (10 A) fuse in the under-dash fuse/relay box.
      -
      An open in the WHT wire.
    • If there is battery voltage, go to Step 5 .
  1. Check for continuity between the No. 6 terminal and body ground.
    There should be continuity.
    • If there is no continuity, check for:
      • -
      An open in the BLK wire.
      -
      Poor ground (G501).
    • If there is continuity, check both mirrors individually, go to Step 6 .
Left mirror
  1. Connect the No. 2 terminal to the No. 10 terminal, and the No. 5 (then No. 12) terminal to No. 6 terminal with jumper wires, and the ignition switch is ON (II). The left mirror should tilt down (then swing left).
    • If the mirror does not tilt down (or does not swing left), check for an open in the PUR (or PNK) wire between the left mirror and the power mirror switch 13P connector. If the wire is OK, check the left mirror actuator.
    • If the mirror neither tilts down nor swings left, repair the GRN wire.
    • If the mirror works properly, check the power mirror switch.
Right mirror
  1. Connect the No. 2 terminal to the No. 11 terminal, and the No. 5 (then No. 13) terminal to No. 6 terminal with jumper wires, and the ignition switch is ON (II). The right mirror should tilt down (then swing left).
    • If the mirror does not tilt down (or does not swing left), check for an open in the PUR (or BLU) wire between the right mirror and the power mirror switch 13P connector. If the wire is OK, check the right mirror actuator.
    • If the mirror neither tilts down nor swings left, repair the WHT wire.
    • If the mirror works properly, check the power mirror switch.
Retractable Power Mirror
  1. Connect the No. 2 and No. 7 (or No. 8) terminals and No. 8 (or No. 7) and No. 6 terminals with jumper wires, and the ignition switch is ON (II). The both mirror should retract (or extend).
    • If both mirrors do not work, check for:
      • -
      Blown No. 36 (10 A) fuse in the under-dash fuse/relay box.
      -
      An open in the LT BLU or RED wire.
      -
      Poor ground (G501).
    • If only one fails to work, check its retractable mirror actuator.
    • If both work, check the power mirror switch.
