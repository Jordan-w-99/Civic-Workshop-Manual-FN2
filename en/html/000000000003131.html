Air Cleaner Element Inspection/Replacement
  1. Open the air cleaner housing cover (A).
  1. Remove the air cleaner element (B) from the air cleaner housing (C).
  1. Check the air cleaner element for damage, dirt, or clogging. If it is damage or clogged, replace it.
    NOTE: Do not use compressed air to clean the air cleaner element.
  1. Clean and remove any debris from inside the air cleaner housing.
  1. Install the parts in the reverse order of removal.
    • If you did not replace the air cleaner element, this procedure is complete.
    • If you replace the air cleaner element, reset the ECM with the HDS and do the ECM idle learn procedure.
