Gauge Control Module Replacement
Tachometer
NOTE: Before replacing the gauge control module (tach), retrieve the ODO value with the HDS
  1. Remove the instrument panel.
  1. Remove the four screws from the gauge control module (tach) (A).
  1. Disconnect the 36P connector (B) from the gauge control module (tach).
  1. Install the gauge in the reverse order of removal.
  1. After installation, rewrite the ODO data.
Speedometer
  1. Remove the instrument upper visor.
  1. Remove the gauge control module (speedo) (A).
  1. Disconnect the 12P connector (B) from the gauge control module (speedo).
  1. Install the gauge in the reverse order of removal.
