Front Grille Replacement
NOTE:
  • Take care not to scratch the bumper and grille.
  • Put on gloves to protect your hands.
  1. Remove the front bumper.
  1. Release the upper clips (A) on both headlights, then gently pull out the upper edge of the front grille (B) to release both upper corners from the clips.
  1. While gently pulling each side of the front grille, release the lower clips (C) on both headlights from the front grille.
  1. If necessary, remove the screws, and release the hooks (A), then remove the front grille molding (B) from the front grille base (C).
  1. Install the front grille in the reverse order of removal, and note these items:
    • If the clips are damaged or stress-whitened, replace them with new ones.
    • Push the clips and the hooks into place securely.
