M/T Clutch Housing Countershaft Bearing Replacement
Special Tools Required

Adjustable bearing remover head, 20−40 mm
  07JAC-PH80100

Bearing remover shaft
 07JAC-PH80200

Slide hammer weight
 07741-0010201

Oil seal driver, 65 mm
 07JAD-PL90100
  1. Remove the bearing set plate (A) from the clutch housing (B).
  1. Remove the needle bearing (A) using the 20−40 mm adjustable bearing remover head (B), slide hammer weight (D) and bearing remover shaft (E), then remove the oil guide plate C.
  1. Position the oil guide plate C (A) and new needle bearing (B) in the bore of the clutch housing (C).
  1. Install the needle bearing using the 65 mm oil seal driver (D).
  1. Install the bearing set plate (A) with the bolts (B).
