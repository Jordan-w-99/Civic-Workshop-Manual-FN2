i-SHIFT System Symptom Troubleshooting - Engine doesn't crank
Engine Doesn't Crank
  1. Turn the ignition switch to ON (II).
  1. Press the brake pedal, and change the shift lever to the N position.
  1. Check the MECHANICAL GEAR STACK FOR STARTER ENABLE in the DATA LIST with the HDS.
Is the starter enable signal OK?
YES -
Test the starter; R18A2 engine model, L13Z1 engine model. ■
NO -
Go to Step 4 .
  1. Check the FOOTBRAKE ON SIGNAL FOR STARTER ENABLE in the DATA LIST with the HDS.
Is the starter enable signal OK?
YES -
Go to Step 5 .
NO -
Do the brake pedal position switch signal circuit troubleshooting; R18A2 engine model, L13Z1 engine model. ■
  1. Check the CLUTCH OPEN SIGNAL FOR STARTER ENABLE in the DATA LIST with the HDS.
Is the starter enable signal OK?
YES -
Go to Step 7 .
NO -
Go to Step 6 .
  1. Check the CLUTCH POSITION in the DATA LIST with the HDS.
Is there more 18 mm (0.7 in.)?
YES -
Check for these conditions, and repair if necessary:■
  • Clutch disc and pressure plate
  • Diaphragm spring
  • Release bearing
NO -
Check for these conditions, and repair if necessary:■
  • Slave cylinder
  • Release fork

YES -
