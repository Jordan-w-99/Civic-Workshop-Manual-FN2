Sunshade Roll Blind Removal/Installation
SRS components are located in this area. Review the SRS component locations and the precautions and procedures before doing repairs or service.
NOTE:
  • Take care not to scratch the sunshade frame, body and other related parts.
  • A blind and an opposite one should be replaced with a new pair at the same time.
  1. Open the blind halfway.
  1. Remove the headliner.
  1. Remove the lower cross-member.
  1. Turn the edges of the blinds (A) over 90 °, then remove them from the shoes (B).
  1. Slide the blinds (A) against the sunshade frame (B) to release the ends of the blind shafts (C) from the sunshade frame.
  1. Remove the other side end of each blind shaft (A) from the sunshade frame (B), then remove the blind (C).
  1. Before installing the blind (A), rotate the blind 360 ° eight times to pre-load the blind.
  1. Push the blind shaft (A) to insert the pin (B) has a diameter of less than 1 mm (0.04 in.) into the holes (C).
    NOTE: Do not remove the pin from the blind until the blind is reinstalled.
  1. Insert the planed shape end of the blind shaft (A) into the hole on the sunshade frame (B).
  1. Remove the pins (A) from the blind shafts (B).
  1. Insert the other side end of each blind shaft (A) into the hole on the sunshade frame (B).
  1. Install the edges of the blinds (A) on the shoes (B).
  1. Reinstall all removed parts.
