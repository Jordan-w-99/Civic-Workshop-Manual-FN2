Clutch Pedal Replacement
NOTE:
  • Without cruise control: The adjusting bolt is substituted as clutch pedal position switch B.
  • Put on gloves to protect your hands.
  • RHD model is shown, LHD model is similar.
  1. Remove the driver's lower cover.
  1. Remove the connector bracket bolt (A) (RHD model only). Disconnect the clutch pedal position switch B connector (B) and clutch pedal position switch A connector (C).
  1. Pry out the lock pin (C), and pull the pedal pin (D) out of the yoke.
  1. Remove the master cylinder mounting bolts (E) and clutch pedal mounting nuts (F).
  1. Remove the clutch pedal assembly (G).
  1. Remove clutch pedal position switch A and clutch pedal position switch B.
  1. Loosely install clutch pedal position switch A and clutch pedal position switch B.
  1. Install the clutch pedal assembly (A).
  1. Install the clutch pedal mounting nuts (B) and master cylinder mounting bolts (C).
  1. Apply multipurpose grease to the pedal pin (D), and slide it into the yoke, then install a lock pin (E).
  1. Adjust the clutch pedal position switch A, and clutch pedal position switch B.
  1. Connect the clutch pedal position switch B connector (A) and clutch pedal position switch A connector (B). Install the connector bracket bolt (C) (RHD model only).
  1. Check the clutch operation.
  1. Install the driver's lower cover.
