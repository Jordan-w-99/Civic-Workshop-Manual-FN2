Headlight Leveling Switch Test/Replacement
Without HID
  1. Remove the instrument panel.
  1. Remove the screws, then remove the switch (A) from the dashboard lower cover.
  1. Measure resistance between the No. 1 and No. 6 terminals and No. 1 and No. 4 terminals at positions 0, 1, 2, and 3 by moving the switch knob.
    Between the No. 1 and No. 6 terminals:
    About 4.7 kΩ
    Between the No. 4 and No. 6 terminals:


    Knob position
    0
    1
    2
    3
    Resistance [About (kΩ)]
    4.2
    3.0
    2.4
    1.9

  1. If the resistance is not within the specifications, replace the switch.
