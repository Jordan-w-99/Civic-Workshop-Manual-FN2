Windshield Wiper Arm/Nozzle Adjustment
Wiper arms stop position
  1. When the wiper arms stop at automatic stop position, confirm that they are at the standard position.
    a:
    Position at about 24.8 mm (1.0 in.) from the top of cowl cover (A)
    b:
    Position at about 91 mm (3.6 in.) from the top of cowl cover (A)

Washer nozzle position
  1. When you turn on the washers, confirm 50 % or more of the washer fluid lands within the spray area. If the spray area is not within the standard positions, adjust the nozzles.
    c:
    Position at about 370 mm (14.5 in.) from the top of black ceramic (B) at lower windshield
    d:
    Position at about 370 mm (14.5 in.) from the top of black ceramic (B) at lower windshield
    e:
    Position at about 158 mm (6.2 in.) from the windshield center line (C)
