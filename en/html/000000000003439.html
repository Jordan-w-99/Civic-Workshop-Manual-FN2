i-SHIFT System Bleeding
NOTE:
  • Do not reuse the drained fluid. Use only clean genuine Honda DOT 4 Brake Fluid from an unopened container. Using a non-Honda brake fluid can cause corrosion and shorten the life of the system.
  • Make sure dirt or other foreign matter is allowed to contaminate the brake fluid.
  • Do not spill brake fluid on the vehicle, it may damage the paint; if brake fluid does contact the paint, wash it off immediately with water.
  1. Make sure the brake fluid level in the reservoir (A) is at the MAX (upper) level line (B).
  1. Remove the bleeder screw cap. Attach a hose to the bleeder (A), and suspend the hose in a container of brake fluid.
  1. Do the CLUTCH FILLING in the ADJUSTMENT MENU with the HDS.
  1. Tighten the bleeder screw securely.
  1. Do the CLUTCH FLUID AIR BLEEDING in the ADJUSTMENT MENU with the HDS.
  1. Refill the brake fluid in the reservoir to the MAX (upper) level line.
  1. Check the clutch operation and check for leaking fluid.
