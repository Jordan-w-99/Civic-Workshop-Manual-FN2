SRS Unit Replacement
Removal
NOTE: If you are only disconnecting SRS unit connector A, skip Step 2 .
  1. Disconnect the negative cable from the battery, and wait at least 3 minutes before beginning work.
  1. Disconnect both seat belt tensioner connectors and both seat belt outer lap tensioner connectors.
  1. Remove the center console.
  1. Disconnect the connector A, connector B, and remove the TORX bolts (C), then pull out the SRS unit.
Installation
  1. Install the new SRS unit (A) with TORX bolts (B), then connect the connectors (C) to the SRS unit; push them into position until they click.
    NOTE: Be sure the SRS unit is sitting squarely against it's bracket before torquing the TORX bolts.
  1. Reconnect both seat belt tensioner connectors and seat belt outer lap tensioner connectors.
  1. Reconnect the negative cable to the battery.
  1. After installing the SRS unit, confirm proper system operation: Turn the ignition switch ON (II); the SRS indicator should come on for about 6 seconds and then go off.
  1. Reinstall all removed parts.
