Ceiling Light Test/Replacement
  1. Turn the light switch OFF.
  1. Carefully pry off the lens (A) with a small screwdriver.
    Ceiling Light:
    8 W

  1. Remove the two mounting screws.
  1. Disconnect the 3P connector (B) from the housing (C).
  1. Check for continuity between the terminals.
    • There should be continuity between the No. 1 and No. 2 terminals with the switch in the DOOR position.
    • With sunshade: There should be continuity between the No. 2 and No. 3 terminals with the switch in the ON position.
    • Without sunshade: There should be continuity between the No. 2 terminal and metallic part of the light.
  1. If the continuity is not as specified, check the bulb(s). If the bulb(s) are OK, replace the light.
  1. Install in the reverse order of removal.
