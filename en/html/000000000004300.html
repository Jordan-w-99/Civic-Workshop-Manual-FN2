Exterior Light Bulb Replacement - Taillight part: Inner
Inner Taillight
  1. Open the tailgate.
  1. Remove the cover (A) from the back of the tailgate.
  1. Disconnect the connectors from the inner taillight.
    Taillight (A):
    5 W
    Rear Fog Light (B):
    21 W (On the driver's side only)
    Back up Light (C):
    18 W
    License Plate Light (D):
    5 W

  1. Turn the bulb sockets 45 ° counterclockwise to remove them from the taillight.
  1. Install new bulbs in the reverse order of removal.
