Glove Box Lid Removal/Installation
NOTE:
  • Take care not to scratch the dashboard and related parts.
  • LHD is shown; RHD is symmetrical.
  1. Remove the glove box lid (A).
    -1.
    Open the glove box.
    -2.
    Release the hooks (B).
    -3.
    Release the glove box damper wire (C).
    -4.
    Release the lower hooks (D), and then remove the lid.
  1. After removing the glove box lid, hold the glove box damper wire (A) by the screwdriver so as not to be drawn back into the hole (B).
  1. Install the lid in the reverse order of removal.
