Driveshaft Dynamic Damper Replacement
  1. Remove the inboard joint.
  1. Remove the dynamic damper band.
  1. Remove the dynamic damper (A).
  1. Install the new dynamic damper.
    NOTE: Be careful not to swap the dynamic dampers. Check the type of dynamic damper by its shape.
