Intake Manifold Removal and Installation

Exploded View
Removal
  1. Remove the harness holder mounting bolt, then remove the harness holder (A) from the bracket.
  1. Remove the positive crankcase ventilation (PCV) hose (B).
  1. Disconnect the exhaust gas recirculation (EGR) valve connector.
  1. Remove the dipstick.
  1. Remove the intake manifold.
Installation
  1. Install the intake manifold (A) with new gaskets (B), and tighten the bolts and nuts in a crisscross pattern in three steps, beginning with the inner bolt.
  1. Install the dipstick.
  1. Connect the EGR valve connector.
  1. Install the PCV hose (A) and harness holder (B).
