Catalytic Converter (Warm-up) Replacement
  1. Without DPF system: Remove the catalytic converter/exhaust pipe A assembly.
  1. With DPF system: Remove the DPF.
  1. With DPF system: Remove the exhaust pipe A.
  1. Remove the warm-up catalytic converter (A).
  1. Install the parts in the reverse order of removal with a new gaskets (B).
