Water Outlet Removal and Installation
Removal
  1. Drain the engine coolant.
  1. Remove the air cleaner housing.
  1. Remove the harness holder (A) from the bracket, then remove the harness holder bracket (B).
  1. Remove the upper radiator hose (A), heater hose (B), and water bypass hose (C).
  1. Remove the water outlet.
  1. Remove the exhaust gas recirculation (EGR) plate.
Installation
  1. Install the EGR plate (A) with a new gasket (B).
  1. Install the water outlet (A) with a new gasket (B).
  1. Install the upper radiator hose (A), heater hose (B), and water bypass hose (C).
  1. Install the harness holder bracket (A), then install the harness holder (B).
  1. Install the air cleaner housing.
  1. Refill the radiator with engine coolant, and bleed the air from the cooling system with the heater valve open.
