Radiator and Fan Replacement
  1. Disconnect the negative cable from the battery first, then disconnect the positive cable.
  1. Remove the battery.
  1. Drain the engine coolant.
  1. Remove the clutch fluid reservoir mounting bolt (A), and remove the clutch line from the clamps (B)(with i-SHIFT).
  1. Remove the front emblem cover.
  1. Disconnect the fan motor connectors (A) and hood switch connector (B), then remove the harness clamps (C).
  1. Remove the upper radiator hose (A) and lower radiator hose (B).
  1. Remove the engine undercover.
  1. Disconnect the engine coolant temperature (ECT) sensor 2 connector (A), and remove the harness clamp (B).
  1. Remove the reserve tank.
  1. Remove the radiator upper bracket mounting bolts (A), then remove the bulkhead (B).
  1. Pull up the radiator, then remove the fan shroud assemblies and other parts from the radiator.
  1. Install the radiator in the reverse order of removal. Make sure the upper and lower cushions are set securely.
  1. Install the bulkhead in the reverse order of removal. Apply body paint to the bulkhead mounting bolts.
  1. Fill the radiator with engine coolant and bleed the air.
