Front Emblem Replacement
NOTE: Take care not to scratch the bumper and emblem.
  1. Remove the front emblem cover.
  1. Remove the bolts.
  1. Release the upper clips (A) on both headlights, then gently pull out the upper edge of the front emblem (B) to release both upper corners from the clips.
  1. While gently pulling each side of the front emblem, release the lower clips (C) on both headlights from the front emblem.
  1. Loosen the bolts securing the front emblem bracket (A) that holds the front bumper (B).
  1. Release the front emblem bracket (A) from the front bumper (B) by releasing hooks (C), then remove the front emblem (D).
  1. Using a T25 TORX bit, remove the screws, then remove the side plate (A) from the front emblem (B).
  1. Using a T25 TORX bit, remove the screws, then remove the front emblem cover bracket (A) from the front emblem (B).
  1. Install the front emblem in the reverse order of removal, and note these items:
    • Before installing the front emblem to the front bumper, tighten the front emblem bracket mounting bolts.
    • Check if the clips are damaged or stress-whitened, and if necessary, replace them with new ones.
