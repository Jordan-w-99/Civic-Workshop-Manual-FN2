ECT Sensor 1 Replacement
  1. Drain the engine coolant.
  1. Remove the air cleaner.
  1. Disconnect the CMP sensor A connector (A), and CMP sensor B (B).
  1. Remove the harness cover (C).
  1. Disconnect the ECT sensor 1 connector (A).
  1. Remove ECT sensor 1 (B).
  1. Install the parts in the reverse order of removal with a new O-ring (C), then refill the radiator with engine coolant.
