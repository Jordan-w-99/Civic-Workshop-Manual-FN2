Cylinder Head Valve Inspection
  1. Remove the valves.
  1. Measure the valve in these areas.
    Intake Valve Dimensions
    A Standard (New):
    31.85−32.15 mm (1.254−1.267 in.)
    B Standard (New):
    116.40−117.00 mm (4.583−4.606 in.)
    C Standard (New):
    5.48−5.49 mm (0.2157−0.2161 in.)
    C Service Limit:
    5.45 mm (0.215 in.)
    Exhaust Valve Dimensions
    A Standard (New):
    25.85−26.15 mm (1.018−1.030 in.)
    B Standard (New):
    115.65−116.25 mm (4.553−4.577 in.)
    C Standard (New):
    5.45−5.46 mm (0.2146−0.2150 in.)
    C Service Limit:
    5.42 mm (0.213 in.)
