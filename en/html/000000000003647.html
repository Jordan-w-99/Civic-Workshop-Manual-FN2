Front Bumper Disassembly/Reassembly
NOTE:
  • Take care not to scratch the bumper faces.
  • Put on gloves to protect your hands.
  1. Remove the front bumper.
  1. Remove the screw and clips, then separate the front bumper upper face (A) and the front bumper lower face (B).
  1. For some models: Gently pull out the headlight washer tube clips (A) from the front bumper lower face (B).
  1. If necessary, remove the screw, the clips, and detach the hooks (A), then remove the lower bumper garnish (B) from the front bumper lower face (C).
  1. Reassembly the bumper faces in the reverse order of disassembly, and note these items:
    • If the clips are damaged or stress-whitened, replace them with new ones.
    • Push the clips and the hooks into place securely.
