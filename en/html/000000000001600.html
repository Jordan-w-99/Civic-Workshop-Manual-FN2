Alternator Removal and Installation
Removal
  1. Disconnect the negative cable from the battery first, then disconnect the positive cable.
  1. Remove the drive belt.
  1. Remove the condenser fan shroud.
  1. Disconnect the A/C compressor clutch connector (A), then remove the harness clamp (B).
  1. Remove the A/C compressor (C) without disconnecting the A/C hoses.
  1. Remove the bolts securing the alternator.
  1. Disconnect the alternator connector (A), BLK wire (B) and harness clamp (C) from the alternator, then remove the alternator.
Installation
  1. Connector the alternator connector (A), BLK wire (B) and harness clamp (C) to the alternator.
  1. Tighten the three bolts securing the alternator.
  1. Install the A/C compressor (A), then connect the A/C compressor clutch connector (B), and install the harness clamp (C).
  1. Install the condenser fan shroud.
  1. Install the drive belt.
  1. Connect the positive cable to the battery first, then connect the negative cable.
  1. Set the clock.
