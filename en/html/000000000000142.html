Engine Undercover Replacement
NOTE: Take care not to scratch the body.
  1. Remove the engine undercover (A).
    -1.
    Remove the clips (B) that secure the front inner fender (C) and engine undercover to the body.
    -2.
    From under the front bumper (D), remove the bolts (E) and the clips (F).
    -3.
    Remove the bolts (G) that secure the floor undercover (H) and engine undercover to the body.
    -4.
    From under the body, remove the bolts (I) and the clips (J).
    -5.
    Release the hooks (K) of the engine undercover, then pull the engine undercover out.
  1. Install the engine undercover in the reverse order of removal, and note these items:
    • Check if the clips are damaged or stress-whitened, and if necessary, replace them with new ones.
    • Push the clips into place securely.
