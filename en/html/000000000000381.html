DTC Troubleshooting: B2375
DTC B2375:
 Infrared Ray Error
  1. Clear the DTCs with the HDS.
  1. Turn the ignition switch OFF, and then back ON (II).
  1. Check for DTCs with the HDS.
Is DTC B2375 indicated?
YES -
Go to Step 4 .
NO -
Intermittent failure, the system is OK at this time.■
  1. Check for loose or poor connection at the automatic lighting/rain sensor 5P connector.
Is the connection OK?
YES -
Go to Step 5 .
NO -
Reconnect the connector, and recheck.■
  1. Visually check the connector damage and terminals condition.
Are there any damage, bent, dirt, and the causes of poor connection?
YES -
Remove or repair the cause, and recheck.■
NO -
Go to Step 6 .
  1. Visually check the windshield condition.
Are there any dirt or damage?
YES -
Clean the windshield, or repair or replace the windshield.■
NO -
Replace the automatic lighting/rain sensor .■
