Dashboard Passenger's Undercover Removal/Installation
NOTE:
  • Take care not to scratch the dashboard and related parts.
  • LHD is shown; RHD is symmetrical.
  1. Remove the passenger's dashboard undercover (A).
    -1.
    Gently pull down the cover to detach the clips.
    -2.
    Pull the cover away to release the hook (B).
  1. Disconnect the ambient light connector (C), and remove the aluminum seal (D) (for some models).
  1. Install the cover in the reverse order of removal, and note these items:
    • If the clips are damaged or stress-whitened, replace them with new ones.
    • Push the clips into place securely.
    • Replace the removed aluminum seal with a new one.
    • Make sure the ambient light connector is plugged in properly.
