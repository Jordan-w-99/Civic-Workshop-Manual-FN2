Sunshade Frame Replacement
SRS components are located in this area. Review the SRS component locations and the precautions and procedures before doing repairs or service.
  1. Remove the headliner.
  1. Remove the sunshade frame (A). Put on gloves to protect your hands.
    -1.
    With an assistant holding the frame, remove the bolts starting at the rear, and release the rear hook (B) by moving the frame forward, then lower the frame to release the pins (C).
    -2.
    With the help of an assistant, carefully remove the frame through the tailgate opening. Take care not to damage the interior trim, body, or seat covers.
  1. Install the frame in the reverse order of removal. When installing the frame, first attach the rear hooks into the body holes.
